Management Systems that make a difference to your business!

Global Connections offer a range of services from enabling our client’s own staff to implement an effective Management System to a fully outsourced solution. Working with the client, Global Connections ltd. develops a documented Management System that not only meets the required International Standards, but also underpins the goals and objectives of the organisation. Global Connections ltd. have experience in a wide range of industries and can use our expertise whether you are involved in design, manufacturing or service activities.

About Us ..?

Global Connections ltd. is a regulatory affairs and quality assurance consulting firm that provides complete business solutions to companies wishing to import, distribute, manufacture and/or market healthcare and food products in Canada. Through extensive experience working with Health Canada and developing regulatory strategies, we obtains optimal product approvals and facilitates successful market launches for natural health products (NHPs), prescription and over-the-counter (OTC) drugs, medical devices, cosmetics and food products. If you wish to sell products in Canada, we will ensure business needs are fully met within timelines and budgets.
